A selective platform for contemporary art.

We work with a small number of artists and gallery partners to present two things: original artworks and limited print editions.

The originals are selected works available through gallery relationships. The prints are limited releases, made from selected artworks, available for 24 hours and never reproduced.

EchoFour sits between contemporary art, collectible design, and drop culture. The idea is simple: take the seriousness of a gallery, the object quality of collectible design, and the urgency of a drop window.

Why four artists

Four is a working ceiling, not a target. We won't take on more than four featured artists at a time. Each artist commits to no more than four prints per year through EchoFour. The cap is there to protect the work, the artist's pace, and the meaning of a drop.

Why four originals

Each partner gallery shows up to four original works at a time on EchoFour. Selected by the gallery, in conversation with us. Available year-round, until they're collected.

Be the first to know.

Originals as they're listed. Prints when an artist is ready. One email per drop, no spam.

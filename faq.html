How does collecting an original work?

Browse Originals, find a piece, click Collect. Title transfers from the partner gallery directly to you on completion of payment. EchoFour acts as the platform and escrow agent.

How do print drops work?

A drop runs for a 24-hour window. The image, title, and edition size are revealed 60 minutes before the window opens. You can join the notify queue any time before the drop. When the window opens, the queue is released in the order you signed up. No payment is taken until then.

Why four?

No more than four featured artists at a time. No more than four prints per artist per year. Up to four originals per partner gallery on the platform. The cap is there to protect the work and keep the platform meaningful.

What happens after a drop closes?

The page becomes archival. The image, edition size, sell-through time, and the artist's note remain visible permanently. There is no second run, no waitlist, and no later release of the same edition.

Where does EchoFour ship?

Worldwide. Originals ship insured via fine art logistics partners (DHL Fine Art or Constantine, depending on origin). Prints ship rolled in archival tubes, dispatch within 4 to 6 weeks of the drop. Shipping rates and timelines are confirmed at checkout based on the work's gallery of origin and your destination.

Can I return a work?

Originals are sold as-described and are non-returnable except in cases of damage in transit or material condition discrepancy. Prints are non-returnable except for damage in transit. Notify us within 14 days of delivery.

What about provenance?

Every collected work comes with a certificate of authenticity from the issuing gallery, signed by the artist. EchoFour also issues a digital provenance record with the full transaction history. When a work is collected, its EchoFour page becomes part of the public record.

Is EchoFour an auction house?

No. There are no bids and no fluctuating prices. Originals are listed at gallery price. Prints are released at a fixed price set with the artist.

Can I submit my work as an artist?

Reach us via Instagram or info@echofour.art. We can't promise we can take everyone on, but we read everything.

Where is EchoFour based?

Meydan Free Zone, Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

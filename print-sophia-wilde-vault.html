Vault is Sophia Wilde's first signed print edition. Made from a 2025 painting in her interior light series, the image was rephotographed in the gallery and reworked in the studio over two months until the print held the same weight as the source canvas.

Each print includes a 600-word printed essay by the artist on the painting and the room it was made in, and a provenance card that becomes part of the work's permanent record on EchoFour.

Sophia Wilde (b. 1989, London) makes paintings of interior light. After studying at the Slade and a residency in Lisbon, she has shown at Blonde Contemporary, London (2022, 2024) and in group shows at Tabula Rasa, Hauser & Wirth Editions, and the Approach.

This is Wilde's first signed print edition, and EchoFour's third drop overall.

The drop opens at 19:00 GST on 15 July 2026 and closes 24 hours later, on 16 July at 19:00 GST. Up to 250 prints will be sold during this window. Once 250 are sold, the window closes early and the page becomes archival. There is no waiting list, no second run, and no later release.

If the window closes with prints unsold, the remainder is offered to the artist's gallery for sale through their channel, not through EchoFour.

Prints ship rolled in archival tubes, signed and numbered, with the printed essay and provenance card. Production begins immediately after window close; shipments dispatch within 4–6 weeks.

Standard transit times: UAE 2–4 days, Europe 7–10 days, North America 10–14 days.

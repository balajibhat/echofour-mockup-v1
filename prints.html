How the archive works

Once a drop closes, its page becomes permanent. The image, the artist's note, the edition size, the sell-through time. We treat each drop like an exhibition catalogue. It should still be readable in ten years.

"A drop is a contract. The artist commits an edition. We commit not to extend it. The collector knows the count. Nothing else needs to be promised."
EchoFour, Drop principle

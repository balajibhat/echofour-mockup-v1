Privacy & Cookies.

Placeholder page. Final policy to be drafted by counsel. The structure below outlines the sections we expect to include and provides representative copy for layout review only. Not legally binding.

1. The short version

We collect the minimum information needed to fulfil orders and run the platform. We do not sell your data. We use a small number of essential cookies and a single privacy-friendly analytics service. You can request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

2. Data we collect

  • Account & order data: name, email, billing and shipping address, phone (optional), payment method (handled by our payment processor; we do not store card details).
  • Communication: messages you send via the contact form or email.
  • Device & usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited — via privacy-friendly analytics, no cross-site tracking.
  • AML data (high-value sales only): verified ID and source-of-funds documentation. Held in encrypted storage, retained for the period required by UAE law, then deleted.

3. How we use it

  • To fulfil your orders and ship your work.
  • To send the EchoFour newsletter (opt-in only, one email per drop).
  • To respond to your enquiries.
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations under UAE law.

4. Who we share it with

Only third parties strictly necessary to fulfil your order:

  • Payment processor (Stripe).
  • Shipping partner (DHL Fine Art or Constantine, depending on route).
  • The relevant partner gallery, for original artwork sales (so they can prepare and release the work).

We do not share or sell your data for marketing purposes.

5. Your rights

You can request: a copy of your data, correction of incorrect data, deletion (subject to retention obligations under UAE law), or that we stop sending you the newsletter. Email privacy@echofour.art and we'll action within 30 days.

6. Cookie policy

We use only the cookies we need:

  • Essential cookies: session, cart, language preference. Cannot be disabled without breaking the site.
  • Analytics cookies: a single privacy-friendly analytics service (Plausible or similar). No cross-site tracking. No personal data.

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or remarketing tags.

7. International transfers

Some of our service providers are based outside the UAE (Stripe, our shipping partners, our analytics provider). Where applicable, transfers are governed by standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

8. Data retention

Order records: 7 years (UAE accounting requirement). AML records: 5 years after the transaction. Newsletter subscription: until you opt out. Contact form messages: 24 months.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy or to exercise your rights, write to privacy@echofour.art.

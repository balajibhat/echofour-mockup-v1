Returns & Refunds.

Placeholder page. Final policy to be drafted by counsel and confirmed with our partner galleries. Representative copy for layout review only.

The short version

Originals are non-returnable except for damage or condition discrepancy. Prints are non-returnable except for damage in transit. We treat damage and condition issues seriously and respond within 48 hours.

Original artworks

Because each original is unique and ships from the gallery directly, original works are sold as-described. We invest heavily in the listing — multiple photographs, videos on request, and a free 15-minute video preview before purchase — precisely so you can buy with confidence.

You may return an original work only in the following cases:

  • Damage in transit: the work arrived damaged. Return shipping is at our cost. Full refund.
  • Material condition discrepancy: the work as delivered differs materially from the listing (size, condition, signature). Return shipping is at our cost. Full refund.

Notify us within 14 days of delivery via hello@echofour.art, with photographs of the issue.

Limited edition prints

Print drops are limited editions sold during a 24-hour window. They are non-returnable except in the case of damage in transit:

  • Damage in transit: photograph the package and the print, contact us within 7 days of delivery, and we'll arrange a replacement from the same edition (if available) or a full refund.

Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 working days of receiving the returned work. International refunds may take longer to appear on your statement depending on your bank.

Contact

Questions or claims: hello@echofour.art. We respond within one working day.

The short version

Original works ship via fine art logistics partners, custom-crated and fully insured to declared market value. Prints ship rolled in archival tubes. UAE delivery is included on originals; international rates are quoted at checkout. Damage in transit is covered.

Originals

Original works ship from the partner gallery via specialist fine art logistics — DHL Fine Art or Constantine, depending on origin and destination. Each work is custom-crated, climate-controlled in transit where required, and insured to declared market value.

  • UAE domestic: 2–4 working days, included in price.
  • GCC: 4–6 working days, quoted at checkout.
  • Europe: 7–10 working days, quoted at checkout.
  • North America & Asia: 10–14 working days, quoted at checkout.
  • Rest of world: quoted on enquiry.

Prints

Print editions ship rolled in archival tubes after the drop window closes. Production typically takes 4–6 weeks; we ship as soon as each print is signed and numbered.

  • UAE domestic: 2–4 working days.
  • International: 7–14 working days, depending on destination.

Customs & duties

For international shipments outside the UAE, the buyer is responsible for any customs duty, import VAT, or other charges levied by the destination country. Original works of art are eligible for reduced or zero customs duty in many jurisdictions; we provide the artist's declaration and value documentation to support claims.

Risk and insurance

Risk of loss passes to you on confirmed delivery. All shipments are insured during transit to declared market value. If a work arrives damaged, photograph the package and the work, contact hello@echofour.art within 7 days, and we'll handle the claim.

Local hand-delivery (UAE)

For UAE buyers within Dubai and Abu Dhabi, we can arrange direct hand-delivery from the gallery, by appointment, at no extra cost. Mention this in the enquiry or at checkout.
